Our Services

Professional Auto Detailing

At KhemsAutoCare, we offer complete auto detailing services designed to clean, restore, and protect your vehicle inside and out. Every service is performed using professional-grade products and expert care.

  • Exterior Detailing

    Complete exterior wash, paint-safe cleaning, waxing, trim dressing, wheel and rim cleaning, and door jamb detailing.

  • Interior Detailing

    Deep interior cleaning including seats, carpets, headliner, dashboard, door panels, and interior shampooing.

  • Steam Cleaning

    Professional steam cleaning for seats, carpets, air vents, dashboards, and hard-to-reach interior areas.

  • Engine Bay Detailing

    Steam-cleaned engine bay with careful degreasing and professional engine dress-up.

  • Paint Correction & Protection

    Clay bar treatment, one-step paint correction, and hand waxing to enhance shine and protect your paint.

  • Disinfecting & Deodorizing

    Interior disinfecting, odor removal, and deodorizing to leave your vehicle fresh and sanitized.
